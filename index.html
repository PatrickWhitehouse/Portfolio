About Me

Hi, I’m Patrick Whitehouse. I am currently a third year student studying Web Development at Manchester Metropolitan University. I have always been interested in the web and how it works. I gained an interest in web development way back when I was in primary school. I was fascinated how I could make my text appear on a webpage with a few lines of code. After I mastered the H1 tag, I then dived into inline styling and again, I was amazed at how I could change the font family and font colour.

After I have completed my third year at Manchester Metropolitan University, I hope to pursue a career in web development, specifically front end development. I thrive off building crisp, clean and professional websites.
